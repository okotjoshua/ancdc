About Us

Our History

1 - Our Journey and Commitment

Since 1984, we've prioritized the socio-economic and spiritual well-being of vulnerable children and households impacted by the Kony war, guided by His Lordship Dr. Bishop Tom Ebraim Okello's vision. Our journey spans from a Compassion Assisted project to an incorporated NGO registered with the UNRB. With evident growth and unwavering commitment, we strive to uplift lives.

2 - Core Focus: Fulfilling Christ's Mandate

Our core focus is to fulfill Jesus Christ's mandate to know, love, and protect every child. We provide educational support, healthcare services, and psychosocial support to empower vulnerable children. Through counseling, mentoring, and spiritual guidance, we nurture their holistic well-being. We advocate for child protection and rights, ensuring a safe environment. Our goal is to equip them with the tools and opportunities for a brighter future.

3 - Comprehensive Interventions for Well-being

We prioritize education, health, psychosocial support, and spiritual development. Through educational support, healthcare services, counseling, mentoring, and spiritual guidance, we empower children to overcome challenges and grow into resilient individuals, equipped with the necessary tools for personal growth and success.

4 - Community Engagement and Empowerment

We actively engage the community, promoting child protection, human rights advocacy, integrated early childhood development, water, sanitation, and hygiene (WaSH), and community economic empowerment. By fostering partnerships and enabling sustainable livelihoods, we strive to improve the well-being and socio-economic status of vulnerable households..

Our Mission

All Nations Child Development Centre exists to holistically nurture children and youth in the community through advocating for their rights so that they can develop into self-sustainable and responsible fulfilled Christian adults.

Our Core Values

Intergrity, Dignity, Stewardship, Excellence, Accountability

Our Main Programs

One

Youth Fathers

The Young Fathers Program empowers young fathers through education and mentorship, equipping them with skills for a brighter future and creating a supportive community for personal growth.

The Youth Fathers Program is a transformative initiative aimed at providing educational support and opportunities to young fathers, empowering them with the knowledge and skills needed for a brighter future. This program recognizes the unique challenges faced by young fathers and seeks to equip them with the resources necessary to navigate parenthood while pursuing personal growth and development.

Through the Youth Fathers Program, participants are given access to a range of educational and vocational training opportunities. These initiatives are designed to enhance their parenting skills, improve their employment prospects, and foster their overall personal development. By addressing the specific needs and aspirations of young fathers, the program helps them build a solid foundation for themselves and their children.

Moreover, the Youth Fathers Program emphasizes the importance of mentorship and community support. It connects young fathers with experienced mentors who provide guidance, encouragement, and role modeling. The program also facilitates peer-to-peer interactions, creating a supportive network where young fathers can share their experiences, learn from one another, and form lasting connections.

Overall, the Youth Fathers Program serves as a catalyst for positive change, empowering young fathers to break the cycle of disadvantage and unlock their full potential. By investing in their education, skills, and personal growth, this program paves the way for a brighter future for both young fathers and their families.
