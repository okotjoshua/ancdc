Job Opportunities

Job Advert August 2023

Details

All Nations Child Development Centre under the Umbrella of Elim Pentecostal Evangelistic Fellowship of Uganda, a Christian organization, is an equal opportunity employer working in Partnership with Impact and Innovation Development Centre (IIDC) to address the plight of children in Lango Sub- region. We are currently rolling out the implementation of Real Fathers (Responsible, Engaged and Loving (Fathers) Programme. (REAL) Fathers) is an evidence-based, community-led mentoring program for fathers of toddler-aged children that teaches positive parenting, non-violent discipline, and early childhood care. With Support from Lego Foundation, the implementation of scale up of REAL Fathers – ECD programme in Lango Sub-region will be implemented in the districts of Lira, Oyam, Dokolo and Kole with main office in Lira District. We are now seeking for a qualified and passionate individual willing to share in our vision and join the team in the following role.

Positions

One

District Technical Coordinator (1 position)

ANCDC (All Nations Child Development Center)
Station:  Lira, Uganda
Reports to: Project Manager

JOB SUMMARY

The Technical Coordinator shall pre-occupies her or himself with coordinating directly with IIDC TA in charge of Scale up and IIDC Project Manager on the quality of the technical aspect of delivery of REAL ECD. S/he must have a full mastery of the REAL Fathers model, methodology and materials take lead in knowledge and skill transfer to the resource persons within the region. The technical coordinator shall be a fulltime staff on the project and shall provide technical supervision to the district project officers and shall work in close collaboration with Project Manager.

ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

Be a constant witness of Jesus Christ with no reproach to the vision of the church, Will be responsible for taking leadership in working with the M&E on measurement of results against the activities and results indicators, Ensure quality transfer of REAL Fathers - ECD knowledge to the district resource teams, technical content delivery of REAL Fathers- ECD materials on the ground in all the districts and identifying gaps and providing technical backstopping to the district officers and resource teams, Take lead in the production and consolidation of both external and internal district program reports and review reports to ensure good quality reporting before submission including monthly briefers, activity reports, quarterly reports, Performance tracking and monitoring to ensure achievement of set targets in the required timelines, Provide timely responses to any donor information requests, Establish QI teams for Continuous Quality Improvement – Monthly and quarterly QI Projects based on gaps identified in implementation, Working closely with the Program leadership to take full responsibility of program risk management by analysing data, anticipating, identifying and mitigating risk factors, Conduct assessment of beneficiary households to identify specific REAL Fathers - ECD needs for program support, Support functionality of REAL Father-ECD coordination structures at district and sub-district levels, Lead capacity building initiatives among REAL Fathers -ECD service providers at various levels.

QUALIFICATIONS, SKILLS & EXPERIENCE

The ideal applicant must hold a Bachelor’s degree in sociology, social work and social administration, community development, ECD, development studies or related courses, At least three years’ experience in ECD/ Fatherhood /community mentorship and district program coordination, ECD/ Fatherhood /community mentorship related work with government institution or NGOs involved in community work, Skills in strengthening health and livelihood service delivery programmes, supporting referrals and linkages between and among communities and health facilities, Strong team Management, documentation, communication and inter-personal skills, Ability to work in a multi-disciplinary team with frequent Movement to the field, Knowledge of the relevant Uganda national Case management and Health policies, strategies and structures, A candidate must be experienced and knowledgeable in fatherhood with track record of marital success with proof of traditional and holy matrimony, Ability to work under pressure with minimal supervision, Have a valid riding permit.
